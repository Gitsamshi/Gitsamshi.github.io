## About Me

I'm an Applied Scientist at Amazon on the **Ads Foundation AI** team, where I work on **LLM post-training** and **agentic mid-training** — teaching large language models to reason, use tools, and act autonomously through reinforcement learning.

My current work spans two threads. On the post-training side, I work on RL-based alignment (GRPO/DAPO/GSPO), reward modeling, and multi-objective optimization for production-scale LLMs. On the agentic side, I initiated and designed [**A-Evolve**](https://github.com/A-EVO-Lab/a-evolve) and am a main contributor to it — an open-source framework that automatically evolves LLM agent strategies (prompts, skills, orchestration) across any domain, achieving SOTA on benchmarks like MCP-Atlas (#1), SWE-bench Verified, and Terminal-Bench.

I've published at ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, IJCAI, ICLR, and TACL, with an **ACL-2017 Outstanding Paper Award**. My research trajectory goes from NLP fundamentals (text generation, word segmentation, sentence embeddings) through vision-language understanding to the current focus: making LLM agents that improve themselves.

## What I Work On

**Agentic LLM Systems** — Designing frameworks where LLM agents evolve their own capabilities. A-Evolve decouples agent evolution from agent architecture via a file-system contract, enabling any agent to be improved through LLM-driven mutation and git-backed gating. The framework ships four reference evolution algorithms and has been validated on four diverse benchmarks.

**LLM Post-Training & RL** — Training large language models to follow instructions, reason, and align with human preferences through reinforcement learning. This includes GRPO/DAPO/GSPO training on large-scale GPU clusters, reward model design, and multi-objective alignment with dynamic reward weighting.

**Vision-Language Models** — Understanding how VLMs attend to visual evidence and when they fail (the "seeing but not believing" problem), and developing inference-time interventions that improve answer correctness without additional training.
